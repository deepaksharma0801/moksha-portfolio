Featured Case Study

Causal impact of remote learning on U.S. student recovery.

A full-stack analytical workflow that merged government education and socioeconomic data, tested causal assumptions, trained an interpretable risk model, and delivered findings through Streamlit.

InputStanford SEDA, CDC, NCES, Census SAIPE joined on NCES district ID.
ProcessingEncoding repairs, zero-padding fixes, subgroup filtering across 44,000+ district-year observations.
ModelingDifference-in-Differences with district and year fixed effects, HC3 errors, parallel trend checks.
OutputStreamlit dashboard with choropleth maps, trend views, model comparison, and at-risk district signals.

Why it stands out

The project does not stop at prediction. It separates causal estimation from risk classification, uses SHAP to explain poverty, urbanicity, and broadband access effects, and packages the results for public inspection.
